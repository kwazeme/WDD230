Target Audience

Temples are secred edifices where members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints perform sacred ordinances for themselves, their families and ancestors. Several ordinances are performed in the temples for which members who attend obviously need dedicated and specialised services including but not limited to a place of temporary residence (a hotel suite or inn), child care services, rooms to change, restrooms, reception places for weddings and family history research centers. As a result of these needs Temple Inns and Suites comes in to provide a level of confort for these patrons so that can comfortably attend to these saving ordinances. Some of the target persona that are cartered for are as follow:

  • Who:Young Couples who are recently married or in the process of getting married and who are in company of immidiate families members. Singular individuals, male or female or church groups who come to worship, serve or attend to religious ceremonies.

  • Age: 14 to 100

  • Motivation:To provide confortable home feeling in a place close to a temple so patrons can safely feel a heavenly comfort as they prepare to perform sacred religious ceremonies in the house of the Lord.
