Target Audience

Manor Chamber of Commerce is a community business promotion and networking organisation. It aims to brings professionals and business owners within the Manor precinct together and assist them in growing their businesses.The target audience of the website web site is described below:

  • Who: Community Business Stakeholders and others interested in doing business in the commuity or in locating their businesses within the community.

  • Age: 14 to 100

  • Motivation: Improve community relations between local busineses and the municipality.

Site Description

The purpose of this document is to describe the TheManorChamber.com website, its purpose and intended audience, utilization scenarios, and other aspects such as its organization and structure, elements of style, etc. It is to be used as a planning and elaboration tool for the personal site to be developed this semester as part of the WDD 230 course.
